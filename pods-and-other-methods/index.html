PODS AND OTHER METHODS

PODS can be applied with other proven frameworks, methods and models by either encapsulating them or being applied within.
Applying PODS will create a container for higher collaboration. Such a "bigger collective brain" can acquire new knowledge, adapt and solve more complex problems faster without management interventions.
